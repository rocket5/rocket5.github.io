We're an award winning game and app development studio founded in 2012 by game industry veterans Catherine Feraday Miller and Tim Miller.

Located in Toronto Canada, we're committed to making innovative and entertaining software that can be enjoyed by children and adults alike.

Our Mission

To create delightful, innovative experiences that bring joy to people of all ages.

We believe in crafting software that is not only entertaining but also thoughtfully designed and accessible.

Our Vision

To push the boundaries of interactive entertainment while maintaining our commitment to quality and creativity.

We envision a world where games and apps serve as vehicles for connection, learning, and meaningful experiences.

Our Values

Innovation, Quality, Creativity, Inclusivity, and Fun are at the heart of everything we do.

We strive to create experiences that reflect these values and resonate with our diverse audience.
